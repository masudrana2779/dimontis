Dimontis Hub

Dimontis Hub is more than an investment product. Ist your entrance card to be part of an upcoming revolution in crypto and finance. The Hub produces Monti. Monti are cryptocoins allowing you to increase your contribution profits based on real entrepreneurial activity and growth of one of the most aspiring digital finance eco systems.

Dimontis Hub is part of the projects extended security design. Dimontis Hub owners run masternodes growing the decentralization and network of the entire ecosystem. Making it impossible to breach.

Furthermore, the Dimontis Hub will be updated over time with additional apps such as allowing you to manage your exchange assets from your Hub to be self responsible and a real owner of your digital assets.

Monti Coin

Fee Reduction

By default, if you hold Monti in your account, your trading fees will be automatically subtracted from your Monti balance - if activated. Using Monti for transaction fees applies a 40% discount to your trading fee. Trading fees are determined based on your trading volume over a 30-day period (in BTC) and Monti balance.

 

Profit Sharing

Crypto company profit sharing is the new standard of investing. Dimontis will share it platform trading and payment services fee revenues with Monti holders.

Holders get up to 50% of the revenue share/bonus of Demontis exchange if they stake their coins.

Be a Early Bird

Be a early bird and buy our Dimontis Hub now to archive the most possible Monti production over time. From year to year the minting functionality will decrease by 50%. So if you wait too long the Hub will produce less Monti, so you get less profit.
